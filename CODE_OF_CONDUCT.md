# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, direct, and respectful. Contributors are expected to participate in a way that keeps discussion productive and welcoming for people with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and precise.
- Assume good intent, but correct problems directly.
- Keep criticism focused on ideas, code, behavior, and security impact.
- Accept maintainer decisions once made.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when they matter.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of issues, reviews, or discussions.
- Security disclosure that creates unnecessary risk for users.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, block participants, or take other action needed to protect the project.

Reports should be sent through the repository owner's private contact channel until a dedicated moderation contact is published.

Maintainers should handle reports fairly, privately when appropriate, and with enough transparency for the community to trust the process.
